# Meeting Prep (OpenClaw Adapted)

Prepare for meetings with structured agendas, participant context, and discussion guides.

## When to Use
- Before important meetings
- Preparing for calls or presentations
- Planning discussion topics
- Researching meeting attendees

## Meeting Prep Template

```markdown
# Meeting Prep: [Meeting Title]

**Date/Time:** [When]
**Duration:** [How long]
**Type:** [Discovery / Demo / Check-in / Planning / Review]
**Your Goal:** [What you want to accomplish]

---

## Attendees

### [Name] — [Title]
- **Role:** [Their role in this meeting]
- **Context:** [What you know about them]
- **Talking Point:** [Something to reference]

---

## Agenda

1. **[Topic 1]** (X min)
   - Goal: [What to accomplish]
   - Questions: [What to ask/discuss]

2. **[Topic 2]** (X min)
   - Goal: [What to accomplish]
   - Questions: [What to ask/discuss]

3. **Next Steps** (X min)
   - Agree on action items and timeline

---

## Context

**Background:**
- [Why this meeting is happening]
- [What led to this]

**Previous discussions:**
- [Summary of prior conversations if any]

---

## Discussion Guide

**Opening:**
- [How to start the conversation]

**Key Questions:**
1. [Question about their situation]
2. [Question about priorities]
3. [Question about timeline/process]

**Potential Objections:**
- [Likely concern] → [How to address]

---

## Success Criteria

Meeting is successful if:
- [ ] [Outcome 1]
- [ ] [Outcome 2]
- [ ] Clear next steps agreed

---

## Materials Needed

- [ ] [Doc/deck/demo]
- [ ] [Data/metrics]
- [ ] [Examples/case studies]
```

## Prep by Meeting Type

### Discovery Meeting
**Focus:** Understand their world, build rapport
**Prepare:** Questions about their workflow, pain points, goals

### Demo/Presentation
**Focus:** Show relevant value
**Prepare:** Tailor demo to their use case, have examples ready

### Check-in/Status
**Focus:** Review progress, identify blockers
**Prepare:** Status update, metrics, what you need from them

### Planning Meeting
**Focus:** Align on approach and timeline
**Prepare:** Options analysis, resource requirements, dependencies

## Quick Prep (< 5 min)

Minimal but effective:

```markdown
**Meeting:** [Title]
**Goal:** [One sentence]
**Agenda:** [3 bullets]
**Ask:** [One key question]
```

## File Storage

Meeting prep can be saved to:
- `/memory/meetings/prep/YYYY-MM-DD-[meeting-title].md`

## Using This Skill

**Input:** Meeting title, attendees, purpose
**Output:** Structured prep doc with agenda and context
