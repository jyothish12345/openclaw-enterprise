## Common Marketing Metrics

### Awareness
- Website traffic
- Social reach
- Brand mentions
- Search impressions

### Engagement
- Bounce rate
- Time on page
- Social engagement rate
- Email open/click rates

### Conversion
- Lead generation
- MQL conversion
- SQL conversion
- Win rate

### Efficiency
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Cost per lead (CPL)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)
